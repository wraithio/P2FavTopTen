Honorable Mentions

Vinland Saga

Description:

Starting off the honorable mentions list is Vinland Saga. It is about young man named Thorfinn who finds himself trying to take revenge against a man who killed his father when he was a young lad. He grows up on the man's ship learning how to fight, pillage, and kill, all while trying to kill him. I love this anime for its ability to bring conflicting characters into a war and shows us that no one is ever truely good or evil on the battlefield. Thorfinn is also so focused on revenge, and fails to see that the man who kills his father is the only reason that he himself is still alive. If you love vikings, action, and character development. This anime is for you!
